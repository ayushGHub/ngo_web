PROJECT OVERVIEW

"Joyful Journeys" envisions the creation of two enchanting rooms, each measuring 20x25 square feet, dedicated to the early education of 1500-2000 kindergarten children from 20-30 villages. This Kindergarten Wonderland will be a space filled with interactive and holistic learning materials, fostering a love for learning and creativity in our little learners

Kindergarten Corner Equipments

  1. Educational Toys: Engaging and age-appropriate toys to stimulate creativity and motor skills.
  2. Storytelling Corner: A cozy space with cushions and a collection of interactive storybooks.
  3. Art Supplies: Crayons, markers, papers, and other materials to encourage artistic expression.
  4. Interactive Learning Boards: Educational boards for numbers, letters, shapes, and colors.
  5. Puzzle Area: Developmental puzzles for problem-solving skills.
  6. Building Blocks: Foster creativity and spatial awareness through building activities.
  7. Audio-Visual Learning: Simple audio-visual aids for fun and interactive learning experiences.
  8. Outdoor Play Equipment: Swing sets, slides, and safe play equipment for physical development.
  9. Educational Games: Board games that encourage teamwork and cognitive development.
